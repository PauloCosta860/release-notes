New in this release

CORE

2358 - when the COH is completed, The process should moves automatically to the billing stage

The process of moving from Confirmation to Billing has been automated and when the COH is complete (Auxilium Signatory and Client Signatory are entered, and the COH document is there).

2375 - Capture details from Passport into Employee Onboarding

Passport can be used to fetch information like First name, Last name, DoB, Passport number, and date of expiry. 




2863 - Employment Contract Digital Signature

DocuSign has been replaced with Adobe eSign feature.
Now internal Signatories & Employees will receive email for signing the Employment Contract form using Adobe E-Signature Process.
A note will be added in Timeline about the status of the document where is at the current state in terms of Signing state.
Once the Employment Contract form has been signed, all participant parties will receive an email with signed pdf Employment Contract in their mailboxes respectively.

2864 - Quarterly Client Satisfaction Survey

From now on, Client Satisfaction Survey will be sent automatically and in bulk to all primary and admin contacts related to the client contract

Customer Service

2856 - Initial Response SLA for cases

Now when creating a Case record from the Customer Services Workspace application, or sending an Email to a mailbox-related email, a response time count will start. The time for this response is given in the Case entity form. Also, in the form you will find a field to indicate that a first response to the case has already been given. If 8 business hours pass without a response, a notification is sent to the owner of the case and to the users who are in the queue or mailbox related to the case.


2857 - Case Resolution SLA for cases

Now when creating a Case record from the Customer Services Workspace application, or when sending an Email to an email related to a mailbox, a time count will start to resolve the case, which is 27 hours (Total). The time for this response is given in the Case entity form. Also, in the form you will find a field to indicate that a first response to the case has already been given. If 23 business hours pass without resolving the case, a notification is sent to the owner of the case and to the users who are in the queue or mailbox related to the case.


2867 - Display Contact and Created date on the Case Card

On the case cards in the Customer Services Dashboard now the customer and the creation date are shown

Fixed in this release

CORE

2552 - Timeline has duplicated entries

To address the issue of timeline having duplicated entries, if the owner and the related client's CSM are the same, only one task will be created. If the owner and the CSM are different, two tasks will be created, each assigned to the corresponding owner. This ensures clarity and avoids redundancy in task assignments.

2683 - Date fields are stored/displaying incorrectly in multiple entities

The Start Date field on the Employment Contract is now timezone-independent, ensuring consistent and accurate start dates regardless of the user's location. This enhancement improves reliability and accuracy in employment documentation across different time zones.

2868 - Cannot proceed from Billing Stage

The field limit for the "Additional Employment Terms" has now been increased to 4000.

2883 - Client Received an invitation to Auxilium Portal

The process to notify the admin contact in the client contract with the portal invitation was modified to just add the admin roles but not to send a portal invitation. This will be in place until the portal development is completed.

2889 - Time entries were only created for 1st June for multiple contracts

Issues with the timesheet entries for the month of June have been fixed.

2921 - Issue with Salt Payroll

The calculation of payroll days is now derived from timesheet entries filtered by employment contract.

3009 - Users don't have permissions to see timesheet tab from Payroll Employee

Now you can access a Payroll Employee record and view the corresponding calendar on the 'Timesheet' tab.

3024 - Issue With Some Invoices

Xero tracking options for SOA,Repa Group, VA Legal, Neeyamo were modified and synchronized with Xero.

2968 - Case Notification not working in PROD

The flows were modified, adding steps to notify users belonging to the “Concierge (Auxilium)” and “Support (Internal)” channels through Teams, depending on whether an email is sent to “concierge@auxiliumservices.com” or “concierge @auxiliumservices.com”, as the case may be or if the cases are added to the corresponding queues.

Portal FL

2884 - Cropped Photos when uploading through FL Portal

It is identified that the problem is caused in the process of taking the Passport Photo field of the Freelance Application to the Employee Photo field in the Onboarding entity. The flow that carries out this process is modified so that within the flow the image is downloaded in full size from the Passport Photo field and then uploaded to the Employee Photo field.
